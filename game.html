How To Play:

You are an elephant who wants to shoot down as many opponents as possible.
Apples give you special power to throw bombs to your opponents.
A green apple gives you a green bomb.
A yellow apple gives you a yellow bomb.
Avoid getting hit by bombs thrown by your opponents and attack them yourself!
Press arrow keys ← ↑ → ↓ to move
Press G (green) and Y (yellow) to throw bombs
